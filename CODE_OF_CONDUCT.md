# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project follows a concise Contributor Covenant-compatible standard: participants must be respectful, inclusive, and professional. Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, threats, doxxing, and deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

## Responsibilities and enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, edit contributions, or restrict participation in response to unacceptable behavior. Report concerns privately to the repository maintainers through the contact method listed in the repository profile. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
